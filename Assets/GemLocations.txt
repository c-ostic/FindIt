Rose Gem
Location: Second to top shelf of the middle cabinet in the Tavern. Right side behind a brown bottle
Hint: To find the Rose Gem, first get yourself a glass of top-shelf Rosé...

Red Gem
Location: Under the burner on the side table in the Alchemy room
Hint: Despite its red color, the Red Gem provides no heat. Someone should tell the alchemist's apprentice, though...

Purple Gem
Location: Behind the candlelit lectern in the Alchemy room
Hint: They say Purple Gems ward away evil sprits. Perfect to keep around a cursed ancient text...

Yellow Gem
Location: On the beam above the door to the Tavern in the Hallway
Hint: Looking for the Yellow Gem? Sometimes taking a rest on a park bench is the best way to take in your surroundings...

Green Gem
Location: Under the wood pile in the Hallway
Hint: It's going to be a cold one tonight. Hopefully the firewood isn't too Green to start a good fire...

Blue Gem
Location: In the cup on the desk in the Bedroom
Hint: I hear the alchemist uses Blue Gems as ice cubes in his late night drinks... wierdo...

Cyan Gem
Location: In the chest near the stack of books in the Bedroom
Hint: The alchemist was never the most organized person. You can find the Cyan Gem in the mess with the rest of his personal belongings...

Orange Gem
Location: Inside the torch beside the staircase
Hint: The Orange Gem was lost the day the apprentice tripped down the stairs. Maybe you are bright enough to find it...
